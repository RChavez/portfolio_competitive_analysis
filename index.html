There is an abundance of information. Images and links are thrown at you. The company wants you to notice the vast amount of choices the retailer has.


Edgy-

A modern look with old-fashioned taste, the site tries to convey that they possess understanding of both modern fashion and vitage appearance.

The store can be organized by article of clothing. The journal uses a two column display which is inefficient because of the magnitude of content present.
